## The Problem

The client (an AI recruitment platform) needed to reach hiring managers at companies actively posting jobs. The challenge: most B2B outreach hits one contact (the Job Poster) and stops there. If that person doesn't reply, the lead dies.

They needed a system that could identify the right people at each company, contact them in the right order, and intelligently escalate when nobody replied, without ever spamming someone who'd already engaged.

## What I Built

A 4-stakeholder outbound engine that runs daily. The system is composed of 11 orchestrated workflows with a master "Run Full Workflow" at the top and a dedicated error handler at the bottom. Each workflow has a single responsibility: scraping, qualification, four parallel email-finder workflows (one per stakeholder role), the 15-path router, the escalation engine, reply capture, and CRM sync. The error handler runs across all of them, catching failures system-wide.

This isn't a single workflow. It's a small automation operating system.

### The Pipeline

1. **Daily LinkedIn scrape.** ~12,000–15,000 jobs pulled per day across configurable hiring signals (job title, location, industry, employment type).
2. **Qualification engine.** Filters scraped jobs by employee count range, employment type, and applicant count. ~400–500 qualified leads per day.
3. **Multi-stakeholder email discovery.** Four parallel workflows find emails for JP, FH, CHRO, and Founder using AnyMail Finder. 77% of qualified leads end up with at least one stakeholder email; 64% have a Founder email.
4. **15-path routing.** Every lead gets exactly one path assigned based on which stakeholders have valid emails. Each path defines a Stage 1 contact (or pair), a Stage 2 escalation, and sometimes a Stage 3.

5. **Campaign enrollment.** Stage 1 contacts are enrolled into one of 4 Instantly campaigns (JP, FH, CHRO, Founder), each with Liquid Syntax conditional variables for peer cross-mentions and escalation references.
6. **Automated escalation.** A separate workflow runs daily, checking enrolled leads for 10-day no-reply thresholds. Escalates to CHRO or Founder per the path's hierarchy.

7. **Kill switch.** Any reply from any stakeholder immediately stops the entire escalation chain for that lead.

### Why This Design

Single-contact cold outreach gets 1–3% reply rates because the right person rarely sees the email. By contacting multiple stakeholders intelligently, with peer cross-mentions and escalation references, the system increases the surface area for response without crossing into spam territory.

The 15-path system isn't arbitrary. Each combination of stakeholder availability creates a distinct optimal sequence. A lead where only the Founder is reachable runs a different campaign than one where JP + FH are both available. Same architecture, completely different message strategy.

And underneath all of this sits a dedicated error handler that catches failures across every workflow in the system.

## The Results

Over a 54-day window:

- 10,753 qualified leads in pipeline
- 18,903 stakeholder emails identified across all 4 roles
- 129 opportunities generated (bottom-of-funnel)

The system runs multi-touch sequences (initial email + 3 follow-ups per stakeholder) across 4 parallel campaigns. Open rate isn't a primary KPI. Apple Mail Privacy Protection has made it unreliable industry-wide, so I prioritize reply and opportunity metrics. The system is fully autonomous on a daily schedule; the client triggers nothing manually.
